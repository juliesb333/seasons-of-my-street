Making of the archive

How the place becomes the piece

01

Collect places close to home

I photograph places around Jamsil and Songpa-gu that I return to often. Some are quiet walks, some are brighter public moments, and each one stays with me for a different reason.

02

Keep memory inside the frame

Many of these photographs include Sulgi and me, so the archive is not only about scenery. It is about companionship, routine, and the emotional texture of being there together.

03

Shape each season as feeling

Color, sound, motion, and writing turn the photos into a seasonal memory map. The goal is not to document one exact street, but to show how nearby places can become personally meaningful.
